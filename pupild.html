student A What I got from Marlborough.

In an academic sense, Marlborough has provided a lot of inspiration which has helped me to determine what I want to do later in life. For example, a talk given by Dr. Chiara Marletto earlier this year about quantum gravity has sparked my interest in more complex branches of quantum and classical physics.

Another talk, this one given by a pupil in my school who has taught himself relativity, was also a great joy to attend. It sticks in my mind as a great example of the supportive and collaborative nature of this subject at its best, which I hope to experience more widely in the years to come when I study physics at university.

Through living in and studying at Marlborough, my confidence has increased immeasurably and has taught me that no aspiration is too high, as long as I work hard enough for it
