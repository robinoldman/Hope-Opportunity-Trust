Who are we?

HOT is a Charitable Organisation whose mission is to change lives and transform the opportunities available for young people that have drive and potential. Our students are young people who have been denied advantage and opportunity but have risen to life’s challenges.

We are offering students fully funded bursary places at some of the finest schools in the UK. These bursaries are funded by the Royal National Springboard Children’s Foundation and direct funding from the host schools. Since 2013 we have realised over £10M of bursary place investment for over 100 students with minimal operating costs. This change has been transformational.
